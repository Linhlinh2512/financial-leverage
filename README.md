# financial-leverage
Conduct an academic research on the impact of factors on financial leverage of construction industry
•	Data sample: The target sample consists of 120 construction companies listed on the Ho Chi Minh Stock Exchange (HOSE) and the Hanoi Stock Exchange (HNX) during the period 2015–2023.
•	Instruments and Tools: Data is sourced from financial databases, including FiinPro and Vietstock, as well as financial reports published by the listed companies.
•	Procedures: Secondary data collection is conducted, involving extracting financial information from company reports, reputable financial websites, and prior studies on financial leverage in construction companies. The data is verified and cross-checked to ensure accuracy.
